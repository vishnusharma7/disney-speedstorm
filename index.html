CHARACTER LINEUP

An incredible lineup of Disney and Pixar racers are suited and revved up for racing combat. Each racer has a unique skill at their disposal. Whether you use Mulan's "Firework Barrage" to blow the competition away or Sulley's "Fearsome Roar" to scare them off the track, there's a wide range of unique skills to master.

ICONIC ENVIRONMENTS

From the docks of Pirates of the Caribbean’s Pirates' Island track to the wilds of The Jungle Book’s Jungle Ruins map, experience these worlds from a fresh perspective geared specifically for racing!
